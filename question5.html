Pink Floyd are an English rock band formed in London in 1965. Gaining an early following as one of the first British psychodelicDefinition: relating to or denoting new or altered perceptions or sensory experiences. groups, they were distinguished by their extended compositions, sonic experimentation, philosophical lyrics and elaborate live shows. They became a leading band of the progressive rock genre, cited by some as the greatest progressive rock band of all time.
